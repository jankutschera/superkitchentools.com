# Keyword Research: Buying Guide Optimization
**SuperKitchenTools.com — US Market, English**
**Research date: 2026-03-15**
**Data source: DataForSEO / Google Ads**

---

## Executive Summary

- 7 of 10 guides target keywords with search volume below 100/mo — the titles are phrased as questions or descriptions, not matching how users actually search
- The biggest wins come from reframing guides around "best X" and "how to" queries rather than "guide to choosing X" constructions
- Guides 3 (cutting board), 5 (stand mixer), and 9 (baking checklist) are critically misaligned — their primary keywords have near-zero search volume
- 5 high-value guide topics are missing entirely: cookware sets (40.5K/mo), espresso machines (33.1K/mo), woks (27.1K/mo), knife sets (22.2K/mo), and nonstick pans (8.1K/mo)
- The knife sharpening guide (Guide 10) sits closest to a high-volume target: "best knife sharpener" gets 22,200/mo — a repositioning opportunity
